Why is encryption important?

Encryption protects data confidentiality by converting plaintext into ciphertext. This ensures only authorized parties can access sensitive information. (Source: NIST SP 800-53)

Can encryption be misused?

Yes, malicious actors can use encryption to hide malware or conduct illegal activities. Example: Ransomware uses encryption to extort victims. (Source: FBI Cybersecurity Report)
